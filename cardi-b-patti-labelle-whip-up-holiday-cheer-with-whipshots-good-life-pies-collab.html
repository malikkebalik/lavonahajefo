    Cardi B has been involved in countless all-star crossovers, but she has now joined forces with the Godmother of Soul for what might be her most unique collaboration yet.

    On Wednesday (November 8), the New York rapper revealed that she is teaming up with Patti LaBelle to combine their respective brands, Whip Shots vodka whipped cream and Patti’s Good Life pies.

    In a promotional video of the duo meeting up to unite their powers, the “Bodak Yellow” rapper tells her new partner in crime that the cream and the pie “have sex together.” After establishing trust between themselves, they try the new blend of flavors for viewers to see.

    In similar news, Snoop Dogg recently launched his own ice cream brand and credited Martha Stewart as well as Master P for helping him on his journey.

    Dubbed Dr. Bombay, the brand is a spin-off of the Long Beach native’s Bored Ape Yacht Club NFT avatar that was born from the West Coast rapper having the munchies and being fed up of mixing different brands of ice cream.
