    Taylor flaunted her beautiful diamond ring. The couple received love from their friends. The two made their first public appearance back in February 2020. The newly engaged couple seems excited to start this chapter of their life.

    What is the age of Taylor Hill?

    Taylor Hill was born on 5th March 1996 in Palatine, Illinois, USA. She is 25 years old at present. She is the daughter of Jennifer Rea and David Glenn Hill. Likewise, she has three siblings Logan Rae, Mackinley, and Chase. Her siblings Mackinley and Chase both are models.

    At a young age, she was a gymnast then she went on to become a model. At the age of 16, she graduated from Pomona High School in Arvada. When she was just 14 years old, she was discovered by a talent agency Jim Jordan at a ranch in Granby, Colorado where she and her family rode horses.

    Two years later, she started her modeling career by being featured in Intimissimi’s catalog. Since then she has not looked back once. Likewise, she has worked for fashion designers like Versace, Chanel, Moschino, Dolce & Gabbana, Balmain, Tommy Hilfiger, and many other brands.

    Workout of the Victoria’s Secret Model

    During the pandemic, Taylor Hill showed what it’s like working out as a model. On her Instagram, she shared her different workouts to tone her legs, abs, and arms. She is advocating for body positivity and overall wellness.

    She wrote,

    “Workout because you WANT to & because moving makes you HAPPY. Not because you think you HAVE TO.”

    Being unable to go to the gym during the pandemic, she worked out from home.
