    Modelling Journey

    All My Children actress Sal moved to Los Angeles to pursue her childhood dream of becoming a model after completing her high school education. At first, she worked as a receptionist to meet her daily needs.

    After winning the season, she signed a contract with Photogenics Model Managemen and appeared in various commercials, including an ad for Wendy.
