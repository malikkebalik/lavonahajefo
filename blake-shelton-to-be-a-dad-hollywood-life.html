    Miranda Lambert Pregnant With First Child

    How exciting!

    Miranda is pregnant with her and Blake’s first child, reports the Aug. 25 issue of Star magazine.

    “[Miranda] and Blake are keeping any baby news a secret for now, but everything points towards Miranda being pregnant,” a family friend told the magazine.

    The same insider reveals that Miranda has allowed herself to snack on occasional comfort food, but has stopped drinking alcohol altogether.

    However, a rep for Miranda tells HollywoodLife.com that the singer is NOT pregnant, sadly.

    “LIES! None of it is true. It’s a completely made up fantasy by Star Magazine once again,” her rep told us.

    Ah, well. Maybe one day we’ll get to celebrate the joys of a Miranda and Blake baby.

    Miranda & Blake Stick Together No Matter What

    Rumors of Miranda and Blake suffering through a troubled marriage have circulated for years, but they’ve stuck together through them all.

    “We had our ups and downs for damn sure along the way,” Blake once said during an interview with CBS, as reported by Us Weekly.

    As far as having a baby, it was previously reported that the couple was ready to start expanding their family in May 2013.

    Both Miranda and Blake are extremely busy right now with their booming careers, but we’re sure when the time is right they will finally settle down and make a baby or three.
