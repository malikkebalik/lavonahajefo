    From falling in love with sports as a child to following her passion in Sports journalism, MJ Acosta's life's been a fairy tale. TheSportster.com rates her as one of the top fifteen Latina sports journalist currently working. She is a sports lover through-n-through. 

    You can see her hosting soccer games, MMA Fights, to boxing matches. Despite being in front of the camera, she rarely opens up about her personal life. We can see her life updates on her Instagram, but she rarely talks about it. If you're curious to know more about her, especially her relationship status, then you're at the right place. 

    NBC's Spartan Host has a Boyfriend!

    Acosta is in love with her Marine. Yes, guys, the sports reporter is in a long-term relationship with her boyfriend. Scrolling through her Instagram, we can find some pictures of the lovely couple together, but the identity of her lover is still intact.

    But we do know that Acosta's boyfriend is a US Marine. She even posted a picture of them together before he was leaving for his country's service. Not just that, whenever he's in town, the couple spends a lot of time together.

    The pair often visits romantic destinations whenever they are together. Before leaving, the couple posted a lovely picture from Napa Valley where they had a lovely time together.

    Is she getting Married?

    Acosta and her boyfriend is head over heels in love. Even though the pair are rarely spotted in public, the pictures of them together speak of their loving bond. 

    Many of her fans want the duo to take their relationship to the next level. Scrolling through her Instagram comments by her followers, we know the fans adore the couple. Despite the fact that the world wants them to get into marital life, the duo, however, has not announced anything regarding their wedding.

    The couple might be thinking about the marriage, but as of now, there is confirmation about the matrimony. We can already imagine that, whenever they will get married, it will be a occasion for celebration. As of now, the pair seems to maintain their romance perfectly.

    Her Journey to NBC's Spartans

    Acosta grew up in a family that loves sports. Her father played basketball professionally for the Dominican Republic National team. She tagged alongside her father to games, and just like that, she fell in love with the sport.

    Growing up, she played lots of sports and also started a cheerleading team for her dad's basketball team. After completing her education from Barry University, she joined WPLG-TV, an affiliate of ABC News. Not just that, she also became a NFL cheerleader for Miami Dolphins in the 2012/13 season.

    With some reporting experience in her belt, Acosta joined Telemundo 20 to cover the NFL teams. She started working for Telemundo 20 from 2016, where she regularly did her reporting work for San Diego Chargers. In her two years of working for Telemundo, a NBC affiliate, she holds the spot as one of the best sportscasters. 

    She joins the list of reporters and correspondents like Newy Scruggs, Lindsay Czarniak, and Andrea Joyce, to name a few. At NBC, she co-hosted a show called NBC's Spartan: Ultimate Team Challenge, which made her more popular among the viewers. As of now, she is the NFL reporter for San Francisco 49ers and Oakland Raiders.
