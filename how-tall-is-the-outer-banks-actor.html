    19 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Now that Outer Banks season 3 is streaming on Netflix, tons of people have swarmed the streamer to see what the Pogues will get up to in the new season. The season 2 finale ended with a surprise reveal of Big John (Charles Halford) being alive, so it’ll be interesting to see how he plays a part in the story’s plot for this season.

    We’ve watched season 3 in its entirety, and let us just say Charles Halford gives an outstanding performance in his portrayal of Big John. We’re not going to spoil anything for the people who haven’t watched the new season yet, but just know that you’ll have a love-hate relationship with Big John throughout the whole season. However, it’s hard to completely dislike him because he’s John B’s father and you can’t help but want them to be on good terms.

    Overall, Outer Banks season 3 was amazing and we can’t wait to see how the story continues in the upcoming fourth season, especially after that surprising ending.

    But while we wait for Outer Banks season 4 to come out, let’s get to know more about the skilled actor who portrays the Big John character. Below, we shared some information about Charles Halford that you might want to know, like his age, height, and more.

    In a 2014 interview with Atlanta Magazine, Charles stated that he was 6 feet 6 inches tall. He’s the tallest main cast member with Drew Starkey, who plays Rafe Cameron, coming in as the second tallest main cast member at the height of 6 feet 2 inches.

    Charles was born on Feb. 28, 1980, making him 42 years old as of Feb. 23, 2023. He will be turning 43 years old in five days. In addition, he was born in Salt Lake City, Utah. His zodiac sign is Pisces, and he has medium-length dark brown hair and brown eyes.

    Charles is best known for his role as Chas Chandler on the TV series Constantine, Earl in Logan Lucky and Sammy Wilds in Bad Times at the El Royale. However, he reportedly has 78 acting credits to his name.
