    Jake Paul and Deji renewed hostilities backstage on Saturday night.

    The old rivals were both in attendance for KSI vs Tommy Fury and Logan Paul vs Dillon Danis to support their brothers, and reunited on DAZN's YouTube countdown show while the undercard was ongoing.

    Jake beat Deji when they fought in a white collar bout on the undercard of KSI vs Logan Paul in 2018.

    However, the Brit did better than expected as he bloodied the American's nose.

    This was both of their boxing debuts.

    Now, five years on, their feud has not been fully settled.

    Deji asked: "Didn't you say I'm your hardest fight? I did bloody your face."

    Jake answered: "No problem, think what you want, you're a son to me."
