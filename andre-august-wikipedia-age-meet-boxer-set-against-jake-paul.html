    Andre August Wikipedia, Age: Meet Boxer Set Against Jake Paul

    Let’s explore the details of Andre August in this Wikipedia article.

    Andre August is a 35-year-old American professional boxer from Beaumont, Texas.

    Further, his debuts dated May 2013, when August defeated Steven Medrano via TKO.

    Since then, the boxer, who stands 5 feet and 10 inches, went on an undefeated run of six fights.

    Moreover, August has fought and won against Patrick Simes, Randon Montgomery, Joshua Kuhn, and Ramiro Gracia.

    Throughout the years, August has contested twelve professional bouts, winning ten with five by knockout.

    Likewise, his only defeat came against fellow American boxer Eric Abraham in 2018.

    Meanwhile, the boxer’s latest victory came by defeating the undefeated 7-0 Brandon Martin, whom he won unanimously on points.

    All of these matches set the record for the cruiserweight champion at 10-1-1.

    Despite the remarkable wins to be mentioned in his Wikipedia, Andre August is relatively unknown in the boxing world.
