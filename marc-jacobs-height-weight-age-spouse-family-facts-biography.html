    Marc Jacobs is an accomplished fashion designer from the United States who has his presence in more than 80 countries. Marc has also served as the Creative Director for another fashion label from France, Louis Vuitton from 1997 to 2014.

    Marc is gay and due to that, he was included in the Out magazine’s list “50 Most Powerful Gay Men and Women in America” in 2012 at #14. He is not afraid to experiment with new styles and says that fashion is for everyone and fashion is identity.

  • Jason Preston (2006-2008) – Marc and Jason had an on and off relationship during their time together. Jason worked as an escort. Jason had inked Marc’s name on his right hand.   • Harry Louis (2011-2013) – Marc dated adult film actor from Brazil, Harry for a while. Harry also runs a chocolate-making business in London. The couple was in a long distance relationship. They tried to balance their individual careers and relationship, by taking romantic breaks together. But eventually, the distance issue caught up with them and they parted ways amicably around October 2013. Harry confirmed their split on Instagram.

They travel together for Marc’s project. The couple resides in West Village townhouse in New York City. On April 4, 2018, Marc proposed to his boyfriend Charly in a Chipotle restaurant. The proposal happened through a flash mob who executed the song “Kiss” by Prince. After a year of the proposal, on April 7, 2019, the couple got married.

    Personal Trainer

    The earlier Marc Jacobs looked very different from the present image. He was lot chubbier before. He decided to take control of his health after he encountered weakness and stomach problem.

    Marc was working way too hard. He saw a nutritionist who recommended joining the gym and changing his diet. Marc followed a two-hour long exercise regimen, 7 days a week. His diet was totally organic with no flour, no sugar, no dairy and no caffeine.

    Marc Jacobs also follows a strict habit of eating 50 grams of oatmeal for breakfast; two chicken breasts for lunch; and juice made of wheatgrass.
