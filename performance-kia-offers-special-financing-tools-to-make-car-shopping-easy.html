    MOOSIC, Pa., Arpil 30, 2018 /PRNewswire-iReach/ -- For many drivers, the idea of purchasing a new vehicle can be daunting and induce a lot of anxiety. Performance Kia understands this conundrum, so to alleviate any possible stress, the dealership provides several financial tools on its website (www.performancekiapa.com). 

    Drivers can begin the shopping process by perusing the dealership's up-to-date inventory of new Kia vehicles, which includes the Rio, Forte and Optima sedans, the Soul, Sportage and Sorento crossovers, the Sedona minivan and the all-new Stinger sports car. 

    Shoppers interested in saving even more on a different vehicle can browse the Performance Kia used vehicle inventory. The dealership's selection includes late-year models from a variety of brands, along with certified pre-owned Kia vehicles and deals under $10,000. 

    Drivers looking for ways to save on a new Kia vehicle can click on the "Specials" link on the Performance Kia website, which will open a list of currently-available deals. These deals may include leases with low monthly payments or financing with low APR rates. 

    To help finance their desired vehicle, shoppers can apply for credit pre-approval through a secure application on the Performance Kia website. 

    Other financing tools available on the Performance Kia website include a monthly payment calculator, along with a trade estimator if drivers are interested in negotiating a trade with their current vehicle. 

    Anyone interested in learning more about the financing process of buying a new Kia or used vehicle from Performance Kia are encouraged to get in touch with the dealership through its website or by phone at 855-638-1337. Performance Kia is located at 4225 Birney Avenue in Moosic.
