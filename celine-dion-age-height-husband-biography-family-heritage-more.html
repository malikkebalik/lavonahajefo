    Celine Dion (born March 30, 1968) is a Canadian singer, actress, performer, artist, businesswoman, and social media personality from Charlemagne, Quebec. Dion initially gained fame as a teen star with a series of French-language albums during the 1980s.

    Furthermore, the 50-year-old star later became the first singer to win both the 1982 Yamaha World Popular Song Festival and the 1988 Eurovision Song Contest . Also, her real birth name is Céline Marie Claudette Dion .

    Celine was first signed to Epic Records in the United States. Also, in 1990, she released her first album in English titled “Unison”. Later, the singer rose to worldwide fame after releasing several best-selling English-language albums such as Falling Into You (1996) and Let’s Talk About Love (1997). Both albums were certified diamond in the United States.

    Also, some of her most prominent songs are “The Power of Love”, “Think Twice”, “Because You Loved Me”, “It’s All Coming Back to Me Now” and much more. On May 21, 2007, Dion released her album in French called D’elles.

    Some of her famous French albums are D’eux (1995), S’il suffisait d’aimer (1998), Sans attendre (2012) and Encore un soir (2016). As a performer, the singer is one of the most successful and respected artists.

    His performances at “A New Day… in Las Vegas Strip” (2003–07) remained the highest-grossing residency concerts of all time. In fact, Dion’s “Taking Chances World Tour” (2008–09) is one of the highest-grossing concert tours of all time.

    Céline Marie Claudette was born on March 30, 1968 in Charlemagne, Quebec, Canada. At present, Celine Dion age 50 years old, as of 2018. The star was born to Adhémar Dion ( father of her) who was a butcher and her mom Thérèse Dion was a housewife.

    Furthermore, the star has a large family with 13 siblings including the eldest brothers Jacques and Michel Dondalinger Dion. He attended the local primary school “Ecole St. Jude” in Charlemagne. But, he later retired at age 12 to focus on his professional career From him.

    He got his first break when his brother Michel sent Dion’s recording to René Angélil (musical director). In 1981, he helped Céline Marie finance her first album titled “La voix du bon Dieu”.

    Furthermore, it later became a #1 local hit and made Dion a star in Quebec. At the age of 18, the artist saw a performance by Michael Jackson. Dion told Angélil that she wanted to be a star like Jackson. Celine also underwent dental surgery to improve her appearance. She also sent to École Berlitz in 1989 to improve her English.

    Personal life, spouse and children

    While mentoring Dion, René Angélil approached the singer. The couple soon began dating in April 1987. They decided to take their relationship further and became engaged in 1991.

    Furthermore, she married her husband Angélil on December 17, 1994, at the Notre-Dame Basilica in Montreal, Quebec. The couple also have three children together. their eldest son René-Charles Angelil was born on January 25, 2001. Later, the loving couple welcomed their twin boys Nelson and Eddy, who were born on October 23, 2010.

    Furthermore, the star also appeared with her newborn children on the cover of Hello! magazine dated December 9, 2010. Her husband Angélil died from complications of cancer on January 14, 2016.
