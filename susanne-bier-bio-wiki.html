    Who is Susanne Bier?

    Susanne Bier is a Danish director, screenwriter, and producer and is best known for her feature films Brothers (2004), After the Wedding (2006), and In a Better World.

    Further, Bier is the first female director to collect a Golden Globe Award, a Primetime Emmy Award, and a European Film Award.

    What are the birth facts of Susanne Bier?

    Susanne Bier was born and raised in a Jewish family on 15 April 1960 in Copenhagen, Denmark. As of now, she will be 63 years old.

    Likewise, she is the daughter of Hennie Jonas Bier and Rudolf Salomon Bier. Bier was raised along with two brothers Jacob Bier, a lawyer, and Philip Bier. Her grandparents moved to different places to escape rising anti-semitism during the rise of Hitler.

    Similarly, she has a Jewish background and has Danish nationality. As for her education, she went to join Den Danske Filmskole, Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Architectural Association School of Architecture, and also Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design.
