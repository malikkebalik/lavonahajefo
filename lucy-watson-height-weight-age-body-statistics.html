    Lucy Watson is an English model, reality TV star, and actress who first gained popularity with her appearance on the TV series, Made in Chelsea. She has also appeared on a variety of other TV shows such as Big Brother’s Bit on the Side, ZTV, This Morning, and many more. As a model, she has done modeling work for brands like PUMA, Nasty Gal, Wilkinson Sword, Bulk Powders, and has been featured in magazines like OK!, Fabulous, HELLO, Grazia, Wonderland, Closer, NOW, and Reveal. Lucy is also the owner of a jewelry brand named Creature Jewellery. She has published several books such as Feed Me Vegan and Feed Me Vegan: For All Occasions.

    Lucy Watson Facts

  • Lucy Watson has launched her own brand of jewelry under the label of “Creature Jewellery”, which features pieces inspired by animal and nature.
  • She became a vegetarian at the age of 6 after her family took her to the slaughterhouse on the farm.
  • She has participated in campaigns against the Down Feather industry and has also worked with pro-vegetarianism foundations.
  • In 2014, she published her book, The Dating Game, in which she used her experience with boys to write a dating guide for young women.
  • She has her own range of beauty care products sold under the label of “Basic Bitch Cosmetics”. All the products in this range are animal cruelty-free and have been approved by PETA.
  • In September 2017, Lucy published a book titled Feed Me Vegan, which was named the “Best Vegan Cookbook” by PETA. She published another book, Feed Me Vegan: For All Occasions in 2018.
  • She launched her frozen food range, Feed Me Vegan, in 2019.
