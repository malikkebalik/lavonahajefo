    Who was Jim Button?

    Jim Button was a true champion of the craft beer scene in Calgary and beyond. He had a vision and passion for local beer that he turned into reality by founding Village Brewery in 2011. He didn’t just stop there, he continued to impact the community with initiatives such as the Circle Carnival festival and the Best of Calgary awards and celebrations. In just a few short years, Jim Button had made an indelible mark on Calgary’s beer landscape, becoming an iconic figure in the process.

    Jim Button was a remarkable man and an incredible entrepreneur. Born in the year 1963, he quickly rose to fame as an expert marketer and businessman in his beloved city of Calgary.     How did Jim Button die? What was the cause of his death?

    Jim Button was a beloved founder of Village Brewery and an inspiring blogger, sharing his experiences on “Gather With Jim”. As if his life wasn’t already hard enough, Button suffered a severe attack of appendicitis back in 2014, which alerted doctors to cancer in one of his kidneys. Doctors were able to remove the kidney, but unfortunately two years later Jim Button was diagnosed with renal cell carcinoma. This Thursday Jim Button succumbed to this disease at the age of 59. He will be remembered fondly by all who knew him, especially those whose lives he touched through his blog, “Gather With Jim”. We honor the memory of Jim Button and extend our sincerest condolences to his family and friends.

    Jim Button was an incredible fighter. He was diagnosed with a terminal illness that the doctors believed would take his life in a year. But the warrior in him fought, and he managed to survive eight more years after the diagnosis. Unfortunately, he passed away yesterday, leaving behind his loved ones and all the people who respected him. His spirit of resilience will live on forever in everyone’s hearts and minds.
