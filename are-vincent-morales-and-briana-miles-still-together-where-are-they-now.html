    Yes, ‘Married at First Sight’ couple Vincent Morales, 27, and Briana Miles, 28 are still in love!

    Vincent and Briana prove that falling in love at first sight on a reality show might just be real. The soul mate is one of the fan-favorite couples of the 12th season of Married at First Sight. The two are the only surviving couple to remain married so far.

    Throughout season 13, there were only three couples who decided to stay married on Decision Day; ‘Ryan Oubre and Clara Berghaus’, ‘Virginia Coombs and Erik Lake’, and ‘Vincent Morales and Briana Miles’.

    However, the other two couples are no longer together.

    Also read “The downfall of ‘Married at First Sight’ couple Ryan Oubre and Clara Berghaus’ relationship!”

    Married at First Sight: Vincent Morales and Briana Miles

    Auto Broker Vincent Morales and Engineer Briana Miles met on the reality show, Married at First Sight which was held in Atlanta in 2021.

    Vincent came into the show to find his dream girl who is ready for marriage as his past partner was not. On the other hand, Miles came to find a compatible partner as well as someone who shares the same core values.

    In their first met, Vincent thinks Briana was bossy as he had struggled to communicate with her. Shortly after, they began enjoying each other’s company and agreed to stay together at the end of the show. The beautiful couple celebrated their one-year wedding anniversary in August last year.

    Also read “Married At First Sight’s Dr. Jessica Griffin is engaged to Jon Francetic!”

    Miles wrote a heartfelt message to Morales in an honor of their first wedding anniversary.

    “To the man who makes me laugh and cry happy tears. To the man who brings me sushi and flowers when I’ve had a bad day. To the man who will go over and beyond to make me abundantly happy and never stops learning new ways to make me smile. Happy Anniversary baby!!!! 1 year down and a lifetime to go!
    Te Amo”

    Vincent makes their big day even more memorable as he got down on one knee with an engagement ring. He asks her to close her eyes at their anniversary dinner table and when she opens them Briana popped the question,

    “Briana, will you marry me?”

    Recently, the couple completed their Los Angeles trip and is looking forward to traveling around the world.

    More on Briana Morris and Vincent Morales

    Briana Morris is an engineer by profession. She is presently serving as an information assurance engineer for a consulting firm. Prior to that, she worked as a program support technician with the Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles.

    Similarly, Vincent is the president of an automotive company and owns a few more businesses. Talking about Morris’s net worth, Morris has an estimated net worth of around $150,000.
