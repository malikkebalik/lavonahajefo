    Who is Peyton Kennedy?

    Peyton Kennedy is a Canadian teen actress. She is best known for her role as Dr. O on the Kid’s television series ‘Odd Squad’ from 2014 to 2017 and has been featured in Grey’s Anatomy.

    She is also famous for her performance in ‘The Captive’ in 2014, ‘American Fable’ in 2016, and ‘Lavender’ in 2016. She also portrayed the role of Kate Messner in the Netflix series, ‘Everything Sucks!’.

    Peyton Kennedy played the recurring role on the PBS Kids television show ‘Odd Squad’ from 2014 to 2017. She made her appearance in several shows like ‘Show’, ‘Hannibal’, ‘ Copper’, ‘The Ron James’, ‘Between’, ‘Murdoch Mysteries’, ‘Killjoys’, and ‘taken’.
