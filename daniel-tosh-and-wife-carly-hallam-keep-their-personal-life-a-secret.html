    People are familiar with the many different shades of Daniel Tosh — he is a popular comedian, producer, author, and TV host. Despite being in the public eye, the comedian is notoriously tight-lipped about his relationships and marriage. 

    Some people consider Tosh to be eccentric, and he found someone who resonates with his choice to keep the personal matters behind the curtains. His wife, Carley Hallam, seems to be a private person herself. 

    Lauded for his black comedy, Tosh is immensely good at his craft. But there have been times when he has found himself in hot waters due to his comic style.

    His comedy show Tosh. O premiered way back in 2009 and is a big hit owing to self-deprecating and dark humor.

    Reacting to the controversies surrounding him, the television host once clarified he was not misogynistic and racist — he just found such jokes hilarious.

    An audience member of his Laughter Factory show once alleged he made some improper remarks about sexual assault during his performance. Soon after, Tosh took to Twitter and issued a public apology in a now-deleted post. 

    The creator of HBO’s Girls and actress Lena Dunham was also not amused when Tosh reportedly commented on her breasts, expressing her unhappiness in a public forum.

    .

    Tosh’s Wife And His Other Relationships 

    Tosh dated model Megan Abrig for many years before calling it quits. He exchanged vows with Carly Hallam, his now-wife. The duo got married in a private ceremony in 2016 but kept the news of their marriage a closely guarded secret.

     

    Hallam is an actress and a writer by profession and is 12 years junior to him. She is a writer for Brooklyn Nine-Nine and her husband’s Comedy Central show. 

    Although Tosh and his wife are incredibly active on social media, they kept the news of their relationship and wedding under wraps.

    The only concrete proof of their marriage was when Hallam’s change of surname appeared in the show’s credit.

    Although she urged the audience to watch out for it, there was no official statement to confirm her marital status. The duo has been also tight-lipped about whether they plan to have kids or not. 

    Tosh has been a strong advocate for legalizing gay marriage. This and the nature of his jokes have led to speculations about him being gay. However, Tosh has a history of dating women in the past.
