    Coffee Meets Bagel Net Worth

    Coffee Meets Bagel is estimated to be worth $150 million as of October 2023. In April 2012, three sisters, Soo Kang, Arum Kang, and Dawoong Kang, founded the dating and social networking service in San Francisco.

    The app seeks to pair a user with a potential partner via a mutual Facebook friend. Discover and Suggested are the two primary sections in which a user can peruse profiles. Coffee Meets Bagel is relatively simple to use and very convenient compared to other dating websites.

    Arum, Dawoon, and Soo Kang, the creators of Coffee Meets Bagel, are three entrepreneurial sisters with distinct educational backgrounds. Arum Kang attended Harvard Business School, Dawoon Kang graduated from Parsons School of Design and is the CEO of Coffee Meets Bagel, and Soo Kang attended Stanford University and is the Head of Design at Coffee Meets Bagel.

    They started Coffee Meets Bagel together, demonstrating their commitment and inventiveness in building a unique and successful online dating platform.

    Company Background and Growth
