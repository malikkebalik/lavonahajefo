    Debbie Allen &amp; Sister Phylicia Rashad Are All Smiles with Their Stunning Daughters in This Snap

    Debbie Allen and her sister Phylicia Rashad are two proud mothers as they posed for the camera with their lovely daughters. The good-looking mother-daughter duos were in high spirits during their get-together.

    08 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Debbie Allen and her sister Phylicia Rashad are two proud mothers as they posed for the camera with their lovely daughters. The good-looking mother-daughter duos were in high spirits during their get-together.

    Talented sisters Debbie Allen and Phylicia Rashad took time out of their busy schedules to spend some quality time with their equally good-looking daughters. Allen shared a glimpse of their bonding time.

    On December 12, she posted an adorable snap on Instagram that delighted her fans. In the frame were herself, her daughter Vivian Nixon, her sister, Rashad and daughter Condola Rashad.

    Phylicia Rashad, Andrew Arthur Allen Jr., Vivian Ayers Allen, and Debbie Allen arrive at the red carpet for "A Tale of Two Sisters," honoring Debbie Allen and Phylicia Rashad in 2018. | Photo: Getty Images

    A BEAUTIFUL BOND

    The photo, which appeared to have been taken in the street, showed the four beautiful women beaming with happiness. They were all in a celebratory mood. Allen captioned the post:

    "Happy Birthday, Chocolate Bunny! Girl’s Trip as soon as it’s safe to fly. Love you, Aunt Deb??"

    Situated at the far end of the snap, Allen wore flat, round earrings with a navy blue top while Rashad at the rightmost part of the picture wore a royal blue top.

    Avid fans of the "Grey’s Anatomy" star were happy to see the sisters' day out with their beautiful daughters. They also sent their warm birthday greetings to Rashad.

    One Instagram user noted that the ladies are "beautiful queens" while others admired how beautiful they are as a family. Some dropped heart and cake emojis.

    The sisters' artistic life will surely flourish as their daughters are following in their footsteps.

    MEETING HER GRANDDAUGHTER

    Allen is not just a doting sister to Rashad, she is also a loving mother to her daughter Vivian whom she shares with former NBA star Norm Nixon.

    She shared that she gushed about Vivien’s daughter, Shiloh Elizabeth Williams, and noted that becoming a grandmother was one of the best days of her life. She added that her granddaughter was "everything" to her.

    ART RUNS IN THE FAMILY

    Award-winning sisters Allen and Rashad, who came from one of the most prominent families in American culture, have been on top of their careers in the performing arts.

    Rashad, who is known for her timeless glow, shared that art has occupied a significant part of their lives and they will continue to pass it on to the family’s next generation.

    Rashad is not only famous for her acting prowess, she is also admired for her long-lasting grace and elegance. The "The Cosby Show" star previously showed off her ageless beauty online.

    She took to Instagram to share a video of herself reading a poem titled "On Aging." Though she appeared makeup-free, she still looked stunning from all angles.
