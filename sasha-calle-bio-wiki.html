    Who is Sasha Calle?

    Sasha Calle is an actress of American nationality. Sasha Calle is popular for her works in projects titled The Young and the Restless, Socially Awkward, etc.

    In 2023, she was seen playing the role of Lt. Aaron Rowland in a psychological thriller film The Girl Who Escaped: The Kara Robinson Story alongside other actors named Katie Douglas, Cara Buono, and Kristian Bruun.

    This project is about the survival of the main character who was abducted by a serial killer.

    In 2020, she was nominated for Daytime Emmy Award, for her acting in The Young and the Restless.
